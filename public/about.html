About Me

My name is Vergi Andriyanto. People call me Andri. I was born August 26, 1986 in Denpasar, Indonesia.

I have spent many years of my life moving from place to place following my dad in pursuing his career. I have lived in the United States for a total of 8 years, and I have moved to many different cities in Indonesia. My family and I moved to the US when I was 3 years old. We lived in Boulder, Colorado. We lived there for a total of 5 years. I went to Martin Park Kindergarten and Elementary School. By the time I was 8 years old, just as I was going into the 3rd grade, We moved back to Indonesia.

In 1994 we came back to Indonesia and I spent most of my life moving from city to city. In 2000, My family and I moved back to the United States. This time we lived in Rockville, Maryland. I went to high school at Richard Montgomery High School
